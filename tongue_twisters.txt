As with many tongue twisters in natural languages, this is NOT good style.</LI><LI VALUE="460" CLASS="TRA"><A HREF="#O103442" NAME="T103442"><IMG SRC="img/up.gif" ALT="up" BORDER=0></A> Sasquatch = Bigfoot in America</LI><LI VALUE="543" CLASS="TRA"><A HREF="#O1000154" NAME="T1000154"><IMG SRC="img/up.gif" ALT="up" BORDER=0></A> Халк Ястреб крушит ястреба, Ястреб Халк ястребит Халка ...
